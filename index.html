Our Prayer Meeting

 

ibelieve hosts a prayer meeting to intercede for Scotland each Saturday at 6pm (UK time) on zoom.

This prayer meeting has been running since February 2021. We are blessed to have people from various nations join us each week to stand in the gap for Scotland. It is humbling to see people from Dubai, Abu Dhabi, India, South Africa, and USA come together to uphold our small nation. Each week we are blessed with worship from Anchorage Church in Dubai along with other guest worship leaders. We are also blessed to have people from various parts of the globe come and share a message with us. It has become a powerful night where God is working among all of us, connecting us deeper with Him and each other. We are becoming a global family.
